A. R. Rahman

Indian Music Composer | Global Icon

Early Life

A. R. Rahman was born in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. He showed an early interest in music and trained in classical traditions before entering the film industry.

Major Achievements

  • Academy Award winner for Slumdog Millionaire
  • National Film Award recipient multiple times
  • Padma Bhushan awardee
  • International recognition in film and global music

Legacy

A. R. Rahman is known for blending Indian classical music with modern sounds. His work continues to inspire musicians and audiences across the world.
